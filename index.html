A Safe Place for Class Ledgers

Attenza was born out of a simple need: classroom attendance is prone to administrative errors, lost paper logs, and system delays.

Our application acts as a secure secondary ledger to record daily class sessions. When professors need to reconcile official sheets or cross-verify data, Attenza provides an indisputable, timestamped backup to assist them.

Important Note

Attenza does not replace your college's official registry or portal. It is a student-led emergency tracking companion helping keep rosters precise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear information on security, installations, and system functionalities.

Is side-loading this APK safe for my phone?
Yes, it is entirely safe. Google Play Console review procedures are sometimes delayed, which is why we distribute the APK directly. This package is compiled directly from the development repository, contains no trackers, and limits authentication strictly to secure email OTP.
How do CR and LR share the attendance pool?
When the first representative (CR or LR) creates a class, they enter the branch, year, semester, section, and roll ranges. When the other representative joins and registers using the exact same class parameters, the backend merges both profiles to manage the exact same data row. They write, edit, and update a single unified pool on Supabase.
Can students modify their attendance data?
No. Students are restricted to read-only access. When a student registers, they verify their roll-number and can see their individual summary dashboard and class history. They cannot change, add, or delete any session logs. Only authorized CRs and LRs have marking keys.
What happens if there's no internet in the classroom?
Attenza utilizes an offline-first sync engine. Representatives can take attendance normally without network coverage; the app stores records locally in the device memory. As soon as the representative connects to campus Wi-Fi or cellular networks, the queue automatically pushes updates to Supabase.
Can students register before the CR creates the class?
Yes! Students can log in and complete their profiles at any time. If the CR has not initialized the class yet, students are held in a waiting state showing a "Waiting for CR/LR Setup" prompt. Once the class is initialized, they are mapped via roll range automatically.
